Why I started Onions?

For some reason, my colleagues and friends were not different from my mum, who somewhat believed that I have a solution to every problem.

For so many years, I was literally, a cathedral of hope for most of the people around me, actively trying to help them with advices, recommendations, solutions – both the ones I had lived through or read through.

It was during one of the moments when I had to stay awake all night to work on a friend’s request, that I realised that there could be so many of my kinds out there.
Why don’t we have a platform where people with tough problems to solve can connect and share with people who know their onions.

Mordi Ochi — CEO Onions Nigeria.
